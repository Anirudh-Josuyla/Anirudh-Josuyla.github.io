About Me

I am a results-driven Data Engineer and Business Analyst passionate about transforming raw data into actionable insights. I believe in the power of data-driven decision-making, open-source collaboration, and leveraging technology to optimize complex processes.

With over three years of experience in data engineering, analytics, and automation, I have designed and optimized scalable ETL pipelines, real-time data processing frameworks, and cloud-based business intelligence solutions. My expertise includes Python, SQL, Snowflake, Databricks, and cloud platforms like AWS and Azure.

During my co-op at Greenhouse Juices Ltd as a Technical Analyst and Sales Associate, I:

  • Designed Power BI and Tableau dashboards, integrating historical sales and customer data to improve forecasting accuracy by 25%.
  • Led data pipeline integration with external vendors, ensuring zero downtime during deployment.

At Tritron Technologies Pvt Ltd, I:

  • Designed and maintained data warehouse schemas (Snowflake, Redshift), improving BI tool performance by 20%.
  • Led CI/CD-driven deployments using Terraform and GitHub Actions, ensuring seamless major and minor releases with minimal disruptions.

My journey into data began during my postgraduate studies in Computer Software and Database Development, where I discovered the power of data to transform decision-making. I was fascinated by how raw data could be turned into actionable insights through machine learning, data visualization, and analytics. This curiosity led me to dive deeper into data engineering, where I found my passion for building robust data pipelines and ensuring data accuracy.

Today, as a Data Engineer, I specialize in designing and optimizing ETL workflows, automating processes, and enabling businesses to leverage data with confidence and innovation. My goal is to bridge the gap between data and decision-making, ensuring that every byte of data contributes to smarter, faster, and more efficient outcomes.
